Apply for Emergency Volunteering Scheme

These prototypes have been assembled very quick and are a communication tool which demonstrates a current understanding of how things work.


Full journey v3

  1. Volunteer applying for Emergency Volunteering Leave

    Thinking: 26th March 2020

    Page 1 - Start page

    We'd like to take a closer look at the content for both the start and confirmation page. Feel it's good enough to put infront of some potential users.

    Page 2 - Tell us about you

    Full name and national insurance number are the unique identifiers to an individual

    Page 3 - DBS certificate number

    Yes and no options for this. Thinking is that we don't want to stop somebody making an application if they don't know where to locate their number. Need clarification on the requirement on this.

    Do we need to state that you must have a DBS number to start placement?

    Page 4 - Current job status

    Where does this information go to? Is it an essential question for receiving a certificate? May need an option to cover "unemployed and recieving benefits"

    Page 5 - Your address

    Should only use this pattern if we know that addresses entered here will be based in the UK. Need clarity if this is the case.

    Page 6 - placement details

    Changed the input field from "postal address" which was one input filed and used the same address pattern we use to ask the volunteer for their address. Consistency.

    Page 7 - Placement duration

    Is it essential the answer to this question is correct? What if, after this transaction, what if the placement changes the start date?

    Page 8 - Placement start

    Telling the volunteer that you can provide an approximate date if they haven't been informed of a definite start date. Need to check requirements about this.

    Page 9 - Volunteer contact details

    Providing guidance as to why we're asking for these details.

    Page 10 - Check answers

    Standard CYA page

    Page 11 - Confirmation

    Need clear guidance what happens and work with content to provide more concrete content and design.

    Email to provider with a link to approve volunteer information

  2. Provider checking volunteer information
    Email to volunteer they have been rejected by provider or Local Authority
    Email to Local Authority to approve volunteer and provider information

  3. Local authority approving volunteer and provider info
    Email to Local Authority to confirm their own approval
    Email to Local Authority to confirm their own rejection
    Email to provider to confirm rejection by Local Authority
    Email to volunteer with their certificate
