You can use this service to apply for an Emergency Volunteer certificate and help your local health and social care services. You will require a certificate to receive compensation.

If you’re employed, you will need the certificate to claim income loss compensation and evidence to your employer. If you’re unemployed, you will need the certificate to claim expenses.

Registering takes around 5 minutes. You can only use this service once.

Before you start

You will need:

  • to know about a volunteering placement within your local community
  • the telephone number and email of your contact for the placement

Ideally, you will have a DBS certificate number from a check within the last 12 months.

There is revised guidance on new DBS checks within the guidance for the Emergency Volunteering Scheme.
