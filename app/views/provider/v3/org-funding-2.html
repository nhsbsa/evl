Confirm your local authority or health authority

Before an applicant can begin their placement, the application needs to be certified by the relevant local authority or health authority of your organisation.

If the organisation you work for:

  • provide social care, you report to the local authority
  • provide healthcare then you report to the health authority

If you are unsure then please choose a relevant Local Authority.

When you submit this information, a point of contact within the relevant authority will be asked to verify the placement.
