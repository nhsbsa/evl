Apply for a certificate to take part in the Emergency Volunteering Scheme

Use this service to apply for an emergency leave certificate. This certificate will mean the following:

  • if you’re working at the moment, you can confirm to your employer that you will be taking time off to take part in the scheme
  • lets you claim compensation once you’ve finished volunteering whether you’re working or not

Before you apply, read the guidance on applying to the Emergency Volunteering Scheme.

Who can apply

To apply, you must:

  • have a specific placement agreed with a care provider, lasting 2 to 4 weeks
  • have worked in health or social care in the past, or have relevant experience

What you'll need

  • your National Insurance number
  • the name, address and phone number of the care provider you’ll be volunteering with
Start now

Before you start

It takes around 5 minutes to apply.

You’re not guaranteed a certificate - the local authority or health authority and care provider will need to approve your application.

If you’re working at the moment you must give your current employer at least 3 working days notice from when you receive your certificate.
