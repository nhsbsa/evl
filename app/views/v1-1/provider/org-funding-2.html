Tell us who the appropriate local authority or health authority is

Before a volunteer can begin their placement, their application needs to be certified. If they are providing social care services they will be certified by the appropriate local authority, if health services they will be certified by the appropriate clinical commissioning group.

When you submit this information, a point of contact within the appropriate organisation will be asked to verify the placement.
